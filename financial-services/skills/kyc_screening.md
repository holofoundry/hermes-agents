# Skill: KYC Screening

Use this skill for onboarding document checks, entity completeness, beneficial ownership gaps, sanctions or PEP review staging, and escalation notes.

Collect entity name, jurisdiction, entity type, registration number, beneficial owners, directors, proof of address, tax forms, risk rating, source-of-funds evidence, sanctions result, PEP result, and adverse media result.

Classify the case as complete, incomplete, needs review, or escalation required. Do not approve onboarding. Escalate if sanctions hit, high-risk jurisdiction, missing beneficial ownership, unresolved PEP, adverse media, or inconsistent identity data.
