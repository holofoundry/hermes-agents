# Skill: Earnings Review

Use this skill after a company reports earnings or releases a call transcript.

Compare reported results against consensus and the existing model. Capture revenue, margins, EPS, free cash flow, guidance, management commentary, segment changes, and drivers. Distinguish reported numbers, company guidance, consensus, and analyst assumptions.

Draft a note with what changed, why it matters, model impact, risks, catalysts, and questions for follow-up. Do not issue a rating or recommendation unless the user provides their firm's approved rating framework and asks for a draft clearly marked for review.
