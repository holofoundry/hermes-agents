# Skill: Comparable Company Analysis

Use this skill for trading multiples, peer benchmarking, valuation ranges, and market positioning.

Collect peer company name, ticker, market capitalization, enterprise value, revenue, EBITDA, EBIT, net income, growth, margins, leverage, and source date. Calculate EV/Revenue, EV/EBITDA, EV/EBIT, P/E, growth, and margin statistics.

Remove peers with missing or non-meaningful denominators from the relevant multiple, but keep them in the data quality notes. Present median, mean, low, high, and quartiles. Explain why outliers are excluded.
